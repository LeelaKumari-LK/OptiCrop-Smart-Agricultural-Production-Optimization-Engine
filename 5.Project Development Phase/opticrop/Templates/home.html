OptiCrop - Smart Agricultural Production Optimization Engine

The main purpose of OptiCrop is to develop an advanced software system that utilizes data-driven insights to optimize agricultural production for different crops. By integrating key environmental factors such as Nitrogen (N), Phosphorous (P), Potassium (K) levels, soil temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall, OptiCrop provides intelligent recommendations to farmers for maximizing yields and resource efficiency.
